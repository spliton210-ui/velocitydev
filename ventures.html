Who I Partner With

I'm selective. Only 3-4 equity partnerships per year. Here's what I look for.

The Founder

  • Domain expertise in the industry
  • Demonstrable work ethic
  • Complementary skills (sales, marketing)
  • Full-time commitment (or going full-time)
  • Has skin in the game
  • Clear communicator

The Idea

  • Solves a real, validated problem
  • Has customer interest or LOIs
  • Clear path to revenue
  • Feasible for AI-accelerated dev
  • Market I can understand
  • Not overly regulated industry

The Business Model

  • SaaS with recurring revenue
  • B2B with clear acquisition path
  • Marketplace with network effects
  • Platform with scale potential
  • NOT low-margin commodity plays
  • Realistic exit potential

The Deal

  • Meaningful equity stake (10-25%)
  • Clear vesting terms
  • Clean cap table
  • Founder retains majority
  • Reasonable scope
  • Mutual respect and trust

If you meet these criteria, we should talk. If not, I'll respectfully pass and may point you to better alternatives.
