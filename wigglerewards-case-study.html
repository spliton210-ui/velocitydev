WiggleRewards Case Study

Enterprise Customer Loyalty Platform with WiFi Integration, AI Support, and Multi-Tenant Architecture—Scalable to Millions of Users

Built in 5 Weeks | Production-Ready | Enterprise-Grade

Project Overview

WiggleRewards is a comprehensive, fully GDPR-compliant customer engagement platform that transforms existing WiFi infrastructure into a powerful loyalty engine. Built on a scalable AWS Lightsail architecture with multi-tenant design and enhanced by AI-powered support, the system automatically rewards customers for WiFi usage, survey participation, and social media engagement. The platform is designed to handle 200,000 to 2,000,000+ concurrent users across multiple tenants while maintaining predictable costs and enterprise-grade reliability.

System In Action

Real screenshots from the production WiggleRewards system showing the admin dashboard, AI assistant, and customer engagement features.

Admin Dashboard Overview

Real-time visibility into customer engagement and system activity. Track member tiers, redemption patterns, and business performance at a glance—giving you the data you need to make informed decisions about rewards strategy and customer retention.

Analytics with AI Support

Real-time feedback on ROI, customer engagement, and venue performance. Track demographics, earning method effectiveness, and tier progression to understand what drives customer behavior. The integrated AI assistant helps you interpret data and optimize your rewards strategy on the fly.

Flexible Earning Configuration

Complete control over how customers earn rewards. Enable or disable earning methods based on your business goals, seasonal promotions, or venue-specific strategies. No developer required—adjust point values and rules through the admin interface in seconds.

Custom Portal Design Interface

Each tenant has the flexibility to design their own user web portal interface using a unique drag-and-drop system. Create branded customer experiences without writing code—rearrange earning methods, feature specific promotions, and tailor the portal layout to match each business's unique strategy and visual identity.
