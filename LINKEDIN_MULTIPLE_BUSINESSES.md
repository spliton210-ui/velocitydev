## üìù Company Page Content Strategy

### How Often to Post?

**Minimum:** 1-2 times per month
**Ideal:** Once per week (if you have content)

**Rule of thumb:** Your PERSONAL profile is primary. Company page is secondary.

### What to Post on Company Page?

**Good content for company page:**
- Major announcements (launching VelocityDev, new services)
- Case studies (when you complete projects)
- Company milestones (first client, 10th project, etc.)
- Industry insights (repost your best personal content)
- Job openings (when you hire)

**What NOT to post:**
- Daily updates (save for personal profile)
- Personal opinions (keep on personal profile)
- Too frequent content (spammy)

### Cross-Posting Strategy

**Efficient approach:**
1. Post content on your PERSONAL profile (primary)
2. Once per month, repost best content to company page
3. Use company page for announcements personal profile can't do
